Clark College of Science and Technology

Welcome to Clark College

Clark College of Science and Technology (CCST) is a private educational institution located in Dau, Mabalacat City, Pampanga, Philippines. It offers a variety of technical and vocational programs aimed at equipping students with practical skills and knowledge.

History

Founded in 2005, CCST has been dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of science, technology, and vocational training. Over the years, it has produced skilled professionals who contribute to various industries in the Philippines and beyond.
